FOR HER

The opulence, Yes! The opulence
Of your love - sweeps me off my feet.
Like a feather in a thunderstorm.
The eloquence, Yes!
Your love IS eloquent.
So cogent.
Like the shiny sun,
Of - the African summer.
Your character, Yes!
Your great character - lights me up
Like a thousand simultaneous glints.
So bitter so sweet - like a mint.
Your smile, Yes!
Your beautiful smile - is a remedy
A remedy; to my troubled soul.
A useful tool - that cleanses me
And makes me feel new.
Your skin, Yes!
Your silk smooth skin - soothes my soul
It is the indication of the GREAT CREATION GOD made.
Your nature, Yes! Yours
Your stature - is perfectly aligned to mine.
I see our future on your lips, only on yours
And every breath you take - takes away mine.
Girl, your nature is breathe-taking.
A masterpiece in the making.
